Our favorite things to do in Austin

We've made Austin our home because it's an amazing city. The people, the culture, and the food are what make it. Even though we love to travel, seeing the world always proves to show what Austin does right. If only it wasn't so hot!

During your stay be sure to take in some of what makes Austin.

Do

Swim in Barton Springs

September is the last month to cool off in Austin's spring fed pool.

Pecan Street Festival

This arts, crafts and music festival will be happeing blocks from the wedding venue.

South Congress

Stroll and windowshop down this street with weird shops, food trucks, and live music.

Walk or Bike the Lady Bird Lake Trail

The hike and bike trail surrounds Lady Bird Lake from Mopac to Pleasant Valley. Alex and Steph both love to hit the trail for a run, when it's not too hot.

Catch a concert at Mohawk, Stubb's or Austin City Limits

Austin loves live music. While you can find it walking around 6th street, these three music venues are our favorite places to catch a show.

See a movie at Alamo Drafthouse

Get dinner and a movie together at the same time. Alamo serves dinner and drinks while you watch the latest releases or attend themed viewings of old favorites.

Blanton Museum of Art

Austin's primary art collection and surrounding grounds are well worth a visit.

Upscale New American. Expensive but worth every bite.
